  • Our History

    Steel Frames Frabricators was founded in 2009 as a mechanical engineering Firm, heavy Steel Fabrication Firm & General building Construction Company and has become a reputable player in the transformation of the Kenyan fabrication industry. The company has two workshops in Ruiru town which is the main head quaters and the other one is Bypass.
